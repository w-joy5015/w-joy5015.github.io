ABOUT

Before my life as a software engineer, I was in the medical field working closely with patients and providers primarily in the research clinic setting, but also as an emergency medical technician. I've always loved thinking outside the box to solve problems whether it be helping a patient with minimal resources or improvising a cooking recipe tailored to the ingredients I have at hand.

I saw programming as a powerful asset in creating solutions for many of our current challenges, not just in health care but also in areas such as sustainability, housing, education, or finance. I'm excited to be able to combine my passion for creating social good with the skills I've developed as a programmer.
